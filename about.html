The Cameo Gimbal is the world's first 5-axis multi-camera gimbal that allows you to capture the world in a spherical field of view, with two cameras that can tilt and pan 360 degrees independently or jointly and with AI software and hardware built in, enabling smart camera coordination and enhanced productivity.

The Cameo Gimbal works just like a chameleon's eyes, having two cameras functioning like the two eyes of a chameleon, covering both the sky and ground. Each camera can zoom in and out, and adjust the focus and exposure, according to the scene and the user's preference. The Cameo Gimbal also has a brain, a computer that processes camera imagery data and commands the gimbal and cameras to execute smart actions optimally. For example, upon detecting certain suspicious targets, the computer drives the gimbal to point both cameras to the subject, then commands one camera to zoom in and the other to zoom out, to get both close-ups and panorama views of the scene.

The Cameo Gimbal is ideal for various applications, such as:

• Vertical inspection: The Cameo Gimbal can double the vertical inspection productivity, by allowing one camera to inspect the top part of a structure, while the other camera inspects the bottom part, simultaneously. The built-in AI can detect objects of interest, such as poles, wires, transformers, insulators, etc., assign the capturing tasks to each camera, point the cameras to the assigned objects, command the cameras to zoom in and capture the required images such that the total execution time is minimal.

• Mapping: The Cameo Gimbal can increase mapping productivity twofold, by doubling the areas captured per trigger.

• Surveillance: The Cameo Gimbal can provide spherical coverage for surveillance, by allowing one camera to monitor the front area, while the other camera monitors the rear area, simultaneously.

• Filming: The Cameo Gimbal can enable one flight to capture multiple shots, such as close-ups and panoramas simultaneously.

• Search and rescue: The Cameo Gimbal can shorten the time to find victims by half, by doubling the search area. In search and rescue where every second counts, cutting the search time by half can mean the difference between life and death.

• Tether applications: The Cameo Gimbal can allow a tethered drone to obtain a spherical field of view, plus the ability to zoom in on any activities while maintaining an uninterrupted overall situational awareness.

The Cameo Gimbal has a weight of 3 lbs and carries a payload of 1.5 lbs x 2. The Cameo carries two full-frame Sony CX90100 cameras or two HeraSight dual EO-IR cameras. It is also compatible with most cameras that have HDMI or Ethernet output and remote control capability. It is easy to mount and operate, with a user-friendly interface and a robust design. The Cameo Gimbal is the ultimate device for capturing the world in a spherical field of view with doubled productivity.
