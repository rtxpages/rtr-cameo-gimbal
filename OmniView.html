Double Duties

One Gimbal - Two Cameras

The OmniView can mount two cameras, one on each side of the device. The advantages of having two cameras are that they can cover both the sky and ground, they can tilt and pan 360 degrees independently or jointly, and they can zoom in and out, and adjust the focus and exposure.

Countless Applications

The OmniView is ideal for various applications, such as:

Vertical inspection

The OmniView can double the vertical inspection productivity, by allowing one camera to inspect the top part of a structure, while the other camera inspects the bottom part, simultaneously.
Mapping

The OmniView can increase mapping productivity by twofold, by doubling the areas captured per trigger.
Surveillance

The OmniView can provide spherical coverage for surveillance, by allowing one camera to monitor the front area, while the other camera monitors the rear area, simultaneously.
Filming

The OmniView can enable one flight to capture multiple shots, such as close-up and panorama simultaneously.
Search and rescue

The OmniView can shorten the time to find victims by half, by doubling the search area. In search and rescue where every second counts, cutting the search time by half can well mean the difference between life and death.
Tether applications

The OmniView can allow a tethered drone to obtain a spherical field of view, plus the ability to zoom in any activities while maintaining an uninterrupted overall situational awareness.
